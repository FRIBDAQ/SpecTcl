Provide a Filter Name

All filters must have a name. The filter name identifies the filter both to you, for future operations, and to SpecTcl. While any characters can appear in the filter name, we recommend that you use alphanumeric characters and periods. Stay clear of characters that have meaning to Tcl.

The buttons at the bottom of the filter wizard have the following meanings:

  • Cancel Cancel the creation of the filter and dismiss the wizard.
  • Help Display this help text.
  • Next Continue defining the filter.
